    CONTEST DESCRIPTION

    • The PEC Code Blitz competition welcomes all students from Panimalar Engineering College, offering a platform for every student to showcase their coding skills and compete with their peers.
    • When you click on the registration button, you will be taken directly to the contest page. This page will provide detailed information about the competition, rules, and how to participate.
    • To participate in the contest, every participant is required to have an account on HackerRank and should set the headline as their Roll Number.
    • By clicking the "Enter Contest" button on the contest page, you will be directed to the challenges page where you can view and attempt the coding problems designed for the competition.
    • The challenges available in the contest are categorized by different levels of difficulty. This ensures that there are problems suitable for beginners as well as advanced coders, allowing everyone to participate meaningfully.
    • To successfully complete a challenge, your submitted solution must pass all the provided test cases. These test cases are designed to verify that your code works correctly under various conditions.
    • After solving a challenge, participants can see their names on the leaderboard. This leaderboard updates in real-time, allowing you to track your progress and see how you rank compared to other participants.
