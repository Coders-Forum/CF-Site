ABOUT
  • The Summer Code Blitz competition invites all students from Panimalar Engineering College to showcase their coding skills and compete with their peers.
  • Upon clicking the registration button, you will be directed to the contest page, which provides comprehensive information about the competition, including rules and participation guidelines.
  • To participate in the contest, each participant must have an account on HackerRank and set their headline as their Roll Number.
  • By clicking the "Enter Contest" button on the contest page, you will be taken to the challenges page, where you can view and attempt the coding problems specifically designed for the competition.
  • The contest features challenges categorized by different levels of difficulty, ensuring there are problems suitable for both beginners and advanced coders, thereby allowing meaningful participation for everyone.
  • To successfully complete a challenge, your submitted solution must pass all the provided test cases, which are designed to verify the correctness of your code under various conditions.
  • After solving a challenge, participants can see their names on the real-time updating leaderboard, enabling them to track their progress and compare their rank with other participants.
