Important Dates

  • 8th January, 2024

    Official Launch

    Are you ready to elevate your coding game? Here's everything you need to know about participating in the PEC Code Quest.

  • 12th Jan, 2024 - 16th Jan, 2024

    Team Registration

    Ready to accept the challenge? Registration is simple! Click here to register for PEC Code Quest and secure your spot.

  • 20th January, 2024

    Preliminary Round  [Online]

    Competitors are presented with a diverse set of problems, testing their problem-solving skills and coding efficiency. Top contenders who will advance to the subsequent levels of the competition.

  • 21st Jan, 2024 - 22nd Jan, 2024

    Shortlisting

    The Top 60 teams in Preliminary Round will be shortlisted and advance to the next level of the competition, i.e Semi-Finals.

  • 23rd January, 2024

    Semi-Finals

    The Semi-Finals becomes a battleground for the top contenders, separating the best from the rest.  The competition intensifies as skilled coders face more advanced programming challenges.

  • 24th January, 2024

    Results

    The Top 30 teams in Semi-Finals will be shortlisted and advance to the next level of the competition, i.e Grand Finale.

  • 25th January, 2024

    Grand Finale

    The Grand Finale is a showcase of exceptional talent, determining the ultimate champion. The participants face a series of challenging coding problems that demand both precision and creativity.

  • Will be Notified soon...

    Award Ceremony

    This event is a moment of prestige, as winners are honored with accolades, certificates, and prizes worth Rs. 10,000/-
